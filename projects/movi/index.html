MoVi

MoVi A Large Multipurpose Motion and Video Dataset

MoVi is the first human motion dataset to contain synchronized pose, body meshes and video recordings. The MoVi database can be applied in human pose estimation and tracking, human motion prediction and synthesis, action recognition and gait analysis. For more information visit the main website
